Vegan Oatmeal Recipe

Description:

Get your taste buds ready for this warm and hearty, easy to make vegan oatmeal! We've combined the simplest most delicious ingredients to create an a breakfast you'll be excited to get up for. It consists mainly of oats, your favourite plant based milk, a bit of nut butter for thickness and to give it some protien, topped with maple syrup, fruits and your favourite superfood seeds. Best of all, it takes less than 5 minutes to prepare from start to finish!

Ingredients

  • Organic Oats (Don't used non-organic as oats are one of the most pesticide laden foods!
  • Jar of your favourite nut butter (we like cashew, but feel free to use PB, almond butter, etc).
  • Maple syrup
  • your favourite fruits such as bananas or berries(we find bananas work best with this recipe
  • Cinnamon to taste
  • a cup of your favourite nut milk. We find organic oatmilk works best, but you can try coconut, almong or soy!

Steps

  1. Boil 300 ml of water or nut milk
  2. add 1 cup of oats
  3. Let boil for a few seconds than simmer on low for 5 minutes
  4. add 2 table spoons of nut butter and stir well
  5. turn off stove, pour oatmeal into bowl and mix in maple syrup to taste (we recommend 1-2 tablespoons)
  6. top with sliced bananas or other fruit
  7. sprinkle hemp seeds, chia seeds or any other favourite seeds on top
  8. sprinkle with cinnamon
  9. enjoy!
